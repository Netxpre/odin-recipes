Chocolate Donut

Here's a recipe for chocolate donuts!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons glaze
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
